Kathfest is the two day annual festival organized by Kathford International College of Engineering and Management. It is an inter-college event for engineering, IT and management colleges, where students get an opportunity to showcase their projects and talents. This annual event will be conducted for two days and will consist of different competitions. We intend to make this event as a flight for the students towards the projects they are aiming. The theme for Kathfest 2019 is “Solving the problems of Nepal”. With the aim of solving the ongoing problems in Nepal through technology and engineering, Kathfest 2019 is being planned.
