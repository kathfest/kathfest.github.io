Software Hackathon

Software Hackathon is one of the pre-events of Kathfest. It is a two-day coding competition, where students come together as teams to build and design something to solve certain problems. The theme for the event will be announced before the event. Based on this theme, the teams should start with an idea, build a prototype that they should present to the jury at the end of the event. Software Hackathon lets participants to think and work under pressure, with different people and gives them an opportunity to work in a real life setting.
